KLOPPR – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

KLOPPR (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is a creator platform that allows users to present their products, link to external shops, communicate, and build visibility. We do not sell products directly and do not process payments inside the app.

1. Responsible Entity

KLOPPR / GRVKN Industries
Email: kontakt@grvkn.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register as a Creator or Viewer, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted via Supabase)
  • Display name / profile information

2.2 Creator Content

Creators can upload:

  • Profile images
  • Product images
  • Product descriptions
  • External shop links

KLOPPR does not store payment data or conduct sales. External links lead to third-party shops.

2.3 Communication

Messages, likes and follows are stored in Supabase (EU region). We do not analyze or sell private messages.

2.4 Device Information

For security & analytics (Supabase), we may collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App usage time & crash data

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used to operate the platform and deliver core functions:

  • Account creation & login
  • Showing Creator pages & product content
  • Messaging, likes & follows
  • Ranking & visibility features
  • Optional premium placements

We do not use your data for advertising outside KLOPPR.

4. Data Storage (Supabase)

All user data is stored and processed by Supabase using secure and encrypted servers.

  • Authentication via Supabase Auth
  • Databases encrypted at rest & in transit
  • EU-region hosting (if selected)

5. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or pass on personal data to third parties.

Data may be shared only if:

  • We are legally required
  • You explicitly request a feature that requires it
  • External shop links are clicked (your browser opens the shop website)

6. External Shops

KLOPPR is not a shop platform. When you click a “Shop link”, you are redirected to the external shop chosen by the Creator.

The external shop is responsible for:

  • Purchases
  • Payments
  • Refunds
  • Order tracking
  • Cookies & tracking on their website

7. Premium & In-App Purchases

Premium features (visibility boosts, VIP Lounge etc.) are billed via Google Play Billing and Pay Pal We do not store or process your payment information.

8. Children’s Privacy

KLOPPR is intended for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Correct account information at any time
  • Delete your account directly in the app

10. Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time in the app. Upon deletion, all personal data will be permanently removed from Supabase.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when needed. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

12. Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data protection, contact us at:
kontakt@grvkn.com
